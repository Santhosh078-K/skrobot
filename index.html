About SK Robotics

“Robotics is not about building machines it’s about building minds."
— Sabarinathan Dhandapani

"Learn by doing – not just watching!" we offer hands-on technical training in Electronics, Robotics, AI, IoT, Embedded Systems, and Programming. Our practical learning approach strengthens core scientific, logical, and coding skills essential for today's tech-driven world. Aligned with the vision of Industry 5.0, our programs help school and college students bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world applications. Through project-based learning, we nurture innovation, problem-solving, and future-ready thinking.. Want to stand out in a tech-driven future? In today’s fast-changing world, students need practical skills that boost critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving beyond traditional learning. These abilities prepare them to adapt and thrive in the future workplace, where technology and automation are everywhere. By gaining hands-on experience, students become innovators, ready to create smart solutions and take advantage of new career opportunities in the evolving Industry 5.0 landscape.
