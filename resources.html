Our Robotics Lab & Components

We believe in learning with real, industry-standard components. Our lab is equipped with a wide range of tools and parts to give students a genuine engineering experience.

Microcontrollers

We use popular and versatile platforms like Arduino Uno and Raspberry Pi to teach the fundamentals of programming and electronics.

Sensors

Our kits include a variety of sensors, including Ultrasonic Sensors for distance measurement, IR Sensors for line-following, and Camera Modules for AI vision.

Actuators & Motors

Students learn to create movement with Servo Motors, DC Motors, and Stepper Motors.

Building Materials

From durable metal chassis to 3D printed custom parts, students have everything they need to bring their ideas to life.
