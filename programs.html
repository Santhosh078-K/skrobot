School Workshops

Empowering School Students with Future-Ready Tech Skills! We provide hands-on training for students from grade 3 to 12 in Breadboard Electronics, Arduino, Automation, Robotics, AI, and IoT for CBSE, ICSE, IGCSE, and Matric schools across India. Our syllabus at SK Robotics is carefully designed to align with the National Education Policy (NEP), emphasizing experiential and interdisciplinary learning. We integrate practical skills in electronics, programming, robotics, AI, and IoT to foster creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving. By blending hands-on projects with theoretical concepts, we prepare students to meet the demands of the modern, technology-driven world while supporting the NEP’s vision of holistic education.

  • Customizable curriculum for different age groups (Grade 3-12)
  • On-site delivery by SK Robotics instructors
  • Focus on hands-on project building and problem-solving
