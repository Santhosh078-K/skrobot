Special Programs & Workshops

School Workshops

We partner with schools to bring robotics education directly to students through customized workshops. These can range from one-day introductory sessions to multi-week modules integrated into the school curriculum.

  • Customizable curriculum for different age groups (Grade 3-12)
  • On-site delivery by SK Robotics instructors
  • Focus on hands-on project building and problem-solving
  • Topics include: Basic Robotics, Coding with Microcontrollers, AI Fundamentals, Drone Building.
